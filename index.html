Perspective

Visibility turns supply chain complexity into action.

In FMCG operations - whether it is a coffee jar in Girona, a confectionery line in York, or a Nespresso capsule in Esplugues - the value chain only performs when many small handoffs work clearly: demand signals, material availability, production flow, packaging compliance, quality checks, logistics coordination, and stakeholder communication.

The opportunity I see at Nestlé is to learn that system from inside while contributing a practical analytics mindset: understand the process, respect the standard, measure what matters, and help teams act faster.
